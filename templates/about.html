About Bangla Dictionary

How to Use?

Here you can easily find your english word bangla meaning.For finding any word you can search on all word here you will show suggestion of your finding like word. You can directly search your word on home page by writing your word and press enter.

Technology used?

It's a simple English to Bangla Dictionary made by python language flask framework and sqlite3 database.

What About Bangla Language?

With over 250 million speakers, Bengali is the seventh most spoken native language in the world due to their population.It has been influenced by other language families prevalent in South Asia, notably the Dravidian, the Austroasiatic, and the Tibeto-Burman families, all of which contributed to Bengali vocabulary and provided the language with some structural forms.
